About Sean

Teaching yoga since 2008

Based in Denver, Sean has spent the last 15 years guiding students through a style that blends vinyasa flow with mindfulness meditation. His approach is accessible and grounding — meeting you exactly where you are.

Whether you're brand new to the mat or deepening an existing practice, Sean creates space for exploration, rest, and real transformation.
